How to ride

1

Sign up

Download the Superpedestrian app. Follow the on-screen prompts to sign up. You’ll need a credit or debit card to get started riding. In some cities, a photo ID is required to verify your age.

2

Find a scooter and scan to unlock

Find a scooter near you by using the map in the Superpedestrian app. Then scan the scooter’s QR code with the app to start your ride

3

Gear up for a safe ride

Before you take off, follow these safety tips:

  • Always wear a helmet
  • Only one person per scooter
  • Never ride under the influence
  • You must be 18+ to ride
4

Get going!

Kick

Step on the scooter with one foot, and push off the ground with the other. The scooter must be rolling to start.

Throttle

Once rolling, use your right thumb to slowly press down on the throttle and accelerate the scooter.

Brake

When you’re ready to slow down, release the throttle and gently squeeze both hand brakes.

5

Park safely

When you reach your destination, find a suitable parking spot!

  • Use the kickstand to park upright
  • Keep walkways clear
  • Don’t block curb cuts, access ramps, doors, or loading docks
  • Keep bus stops clear
